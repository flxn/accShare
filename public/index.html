About AccShare

What?

AccShare is a service to share Account informations with the rest of the world.

Why?

The internet is full of login forms. Nowadays, almost every site requires you to register. No matter if you just want to test it out, view an attachment on a forum or get access to some content you need.

In my opinion that's annoying.

I don't want to give out my name, birthday and email address to some random website owner I don't know and you probably won't either... but sadly it's necessary.

How?

AccShare tries to solve this problem by providing a platform to share fake login informations.

The site relies heavily on an active community to reach it's goal of a hassel free internet but it's super easy to participate:

If there are no credentials for a site you need create a fake account and add it to AccShare to help others
(But please comply with the Terms Of Use when doing so).
A small step for you a huge slightly bigger leap for an open internet.

When you like this idea please spread this site. The more people participate the better.
